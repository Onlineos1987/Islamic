Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for Umrah visa?

You will need:

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months
  • Two recent passport-sized photos with white background
  • Completed visa application form
  • Proof of vaccination (meningitis and COVID-19)
  • Confirmed round-trip airline tickets
  • Hotel reservation in Makkah and Madinah
How early should I apply for Umrah?

We recommend applying at least 60 days before your intended travel date. This allows sufficient time for visa processing and ensures you get your preferred travel dates and accommodations. During peak seasons (Ramadan, Hajj season, school holidays), we advise applying 90 days in advance.

What is included in your packages?

All our packages include:

  • Round-trip airfare from your departure city
  • Accommodation in Makkah and Madinah
  • All ground transportation in Saudi Arabia
  • Ziyarat (religious site visits) with knowledgeable guides
  • Visa processing assistance
  • 24/7 customer support in Saudi Arabia

VIP packages include additional services like Haram-view rooms, premium transportation, and personal guides.

Can I perform Umrah during Hajj season?

Umrah visas are not issued during the Hajj season (typically from the beginning of Shawwal until the 10th of Dhul-Hijjah). However, you can perform Umrah before Hajj season begins or after it concludes. We offer special packages for the pre and post-Hajj seasons.

What COVID-19 precautions are in place?

We follow all Saudi Ministry of Health guidelines:

  • Vaccination certificate required for all travelers
  • Regular sanitization of vehicles and hotels
  • Social distancing measures during group activities
  • Masks provided for all travelers
  • Temperature checks at hotels and transport points
