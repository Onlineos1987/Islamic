Umrah Policies After Hajj 2025

  • 🕋 Umrah resumes: Officially begins after 15 Dhul Hijjah 1446 (approx. mid-June 2025)
  • 🌍 Eligibility: All Muslims aged 12+ with valid passports and vaccines
  • 📅 Booking time: Bookings open from July 1st, 2025 for early August departures
  • 💉 Vaccines required: Meningitis, COVID-19 (latest WHO approved), and Flu shots
  • 📱 Mandatory App: Nusuk app required for Umrah permit generation and tracking
  • 🏨 Accommodation rules: Max 4 people per hotel room for economy class; VIP packages offer double sharing
  • ✈️ Flights & visas: Valid for 90 days; multiple-entry Umrah eVisas now supported
  • 🕌 Women travel: Women of all ages may travel without a mahram if in group tours
  • 📸 Dress code: Abayas not mandatory for non-Saudi women, but respectful clothing is advised
  • 🕐 Stay duration: 10–30 days depending on package; extensions available with VIP visa
