Step-by-Step Umrah Guide

  1. Get your documents ready: Passport, photos, vaccine certificate.
  2. Departure & Ihram: Enter state of Ihram from Miqat.
  3. Upon arrival: Perform Tawaf and Sa’i in Masjid al-Haram.
  4. Shaving/trimming hair to exit Ihram.
  5. Ziyarat of Madinah and Prophet’s Mosque (optional).
