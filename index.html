Step-by-Step Umrah Guide

  1. Before Departure: Apply for visa, take vaccinations, prepare Ihram.
  2. Arrival in Makkah: Perform Tawaf, Sa'i between Safa and Marwa, Tahallul.
  3. Madinah Visit: Optional but highly recommended for Ziyarat.
  4. Important Tips: Keep documents safe, follow Islamic dress code, avoid rush.
