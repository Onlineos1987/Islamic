Umrah Policies After Hajj 2025

  • Vaccination certificate (COVID-19, Meningitis, Flu) is mandatory.
  • Biometric verification required before visa processing.
  • Women allowed to travel without Mehram under licensed groups.
  • Children under 2 years exempt from visa fees. Above 2 require full fare.
  • All visa and travel bookings are subject to Saudi Hajj Ministry updates.
  • Bookings are refundable only in case of visa rejection by embassy.
