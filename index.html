Umrah Policies (Post Hajj 2025)

  • Only pilgrims above 18 years allowed unless with family.
  • Mandatory digital vaccine certificate (MenACWY, COVID-19).
  • Women can now perform Umrah without Mahram (select packages only).
  • Each passport must have minimum 6 months validity.
  • Bookings for Umrah open from 1st Muharram 1447H.
  • Stay duration limited to 30 days per visa as per Saudi regulations.
